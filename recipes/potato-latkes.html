Mom's Potato Latkes

Description

This wonderful and heart warming recipe is a must for the holiday season they will disapper as quick as you can make them. Great option for hanukkah, chrismis, yule, or any ohter holiday you celebrate. Potato latkes also know as potato pancakes they can be served hot or cold and with many kinds of conboments, apple sause is a great start. this mothers recipe is the one i always end up coming back to after trying others.

ingredients

    - 3 cups shredded potato (hash browns) - 1/4 cup grated onion -2 large eggs, beaten -6 saltine crackers, or as needed -1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste -1/4 tsp black pepper - 1/2 cup vegetable oil, or as needed

Steps

    -Mix potatos,onion,egg,crushed crackers,salt,and pepper together in a large bowl -Heat 1/4 inch oil in a heavy skillet over medium-high heat -Get spoonfuls of the potato mixture, press mixture against side of bowl to remove excess liquid, drop mixture into hot oil, slightly flatten the latkes into the oil with the back of the spoon. -Cook in batches in the hot oil untill golden brown and crisp 3 to 5 minutes per side, drain latkes on a paper towel-lined plate. -Enjoy!
